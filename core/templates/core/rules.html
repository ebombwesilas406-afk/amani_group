Rules & Guidelines

Our Commitment to a Supportive and Transparent Community

Group Expectations

Treat all members with respect and dignity.
Participate actively in group activities and support programs.
Maintain confidentiality for sensitive matters.

Member Conduct

Attend meetings and community events when possible.
Contribute to initiatives like funeral and emergency aid.
Actively encourage and assist others in times of need.

Values Enforcement

Unity Trust reserves the right to review member conduct to ensure alignment with our mission. Violations of core principles may result in warnings or, in rare cases, removal from the group to protect our community’s integrity.
