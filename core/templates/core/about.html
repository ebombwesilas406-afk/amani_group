About Unity Trust

Our Story, Our Mission, and Our Future

Our Story

Unity Trust began with a vision to create a safe and supportive community where individuals could rely on one another in times of need.

We recognized that many young leaders and community members faced challenges without a support system, and we wanted to change that. Founded by passionate community leaders, we've grown into a trusted organization serving hundreds across Western Kenya.

Our Mission

To provide emotional, financial, and emergency support to members while fostering a culture of solidarity and community responsibility.

Our Vision

A Western Kenya where every member of our community feels supported, valued, and empowered to thrive.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We care deeply about every member's well-being.

Solidarity

Together, we are stronger and more resilient.

Integrity

Transparency and honesty guide all our actions.

Responsibility

Members contribute to and uphold the group's mission.
